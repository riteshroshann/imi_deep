\begin{abstract}
Carbon fiber reinforced polymer (CFRP) composites are critical structural materials in aerospace, automotive, and renewable energy applications, yet their fatigue-driven degradation remains mathematically challenging to predict and optimize. This paper presents an integrated artificial intelligence framework that unifies high-dimensional signal processing, Physics-Informed Neural Networks (PINNs), a novel Hybrid Spatio-Temporal Attention (HybridSTA) sequence mechanism, and Bayesian Pareto Optimization to predict Remaining Useful Life (RUL) and autonomously optimize layup configurations for structural damage tolerance. We distill over 332,000 raw actuator-sensor measurements (NASA PCoE continuous tension-tension dataset) into a highly regularized dense feature tensor capturing Time-of-Flight, variances, and acoustic cross-correlations. Evaluating architectures from Temporal Convolutional Networks (TCN) to BiLSTMs against authentic, unaugmented material deterioration profiles, we demonstrate that our novel HybridSTA block---which incorporates Squeeze-and-Excitation channel gating paired with geometric 2D sensor layout positional attention---achieves superior degradation prediction, establishing a state-of-the-art $R^2 = 0.826$ while utilizing $90\%$ fewer parameters than leading recurrent networks. Further, a Physics-Informed embedding kinetically enforces the \textit{Paris Law} crack growth equation directly into the loss manifold, reducing critical RUL extrapolation error by 23\% during late-stage failure regimes. Epistemic uncertainties are bound using distribution-free Conformal Prediction, yielding rigorous $90\%$ survival coverage intervals. Using Explainable AI, we decode the HybridSTA self-attention weights to trace physical inter-laminar acoustic decoupling gracefully. Finally, a Gaussian Process surrogate scalarizing multi-objective Expected Improvement (EI) identifies optimal layups (e.g., $[0/45/90/-45]_{2s}$) that improve expected RUL by 18--27\% while maintaining strict stiffness constraints. Code and datasets are open-sourced for mathematical reproducibility.
\end{abstract}

\section{Introduction}
%═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Carbon fiber reinforced polymer (CFRP) composites have become indispensable in high-performance engineering applications---from commercial aircraft fuselages to Formula~1 monocoques---owing to their exceptional specific stiffness, strength, and fatigue resistance \cite{mouritz2012review}. However, unlike metallic structures where mode-I crack propagation follows well-characterized fracture mechanics, CFRP composites exhibit complex, multi-scale, and non-linear damage mechanisms: matrix microcracking initiates at relatively low load regimes, followed by inter-laminar delamination onset, and culminating in catastrophic, sudden fiber breakage \cite{reifsnider1991damage}.

The high dimensionality of structural sensor feedback---specifically 16 piezoelectric transducers (PZT) generating active Lamb wave acoustic signals at a $1\,\text{MHz}$ sampling rate---combined with the necessity to bridge governing physical differential equations with Deep Learning (DL), establishes a formidable computational environment. Contemporary prognostic approaches predominantly employ isolated Machine Learning (ML) regressors (e.g., Random Forests, XGBoost) that mathematically ignore the rich physics of composite fatigue kinematics \cite{khan2019damage,loutas2017data}.

In this paper, we bridge this structural informatics gap by presenting a meticulously architected, multi-modal pipeline capable of robust interpretation and stochastic optimization. The primary contributions of this work are encapsulated as follows:

\begin{enumerate}
    \item \textbf{Acoustic Topological Distillation}: Systematic extraction and fusion of multi-modal domains and Damage Index criteria from 16 interconnected hardware channels—condensing 332,388 raw unadulterated NASA waveform paths into an optimized, robust 17-channel input matrix resilient to sensor masking and noise.
    \item \textbf{Novel HybridSTA Architecture}: The theoretical conception and empirical validation of a Hybrid Spatio-Temporal Attention topology. By explicitly decoupling $1\times1$ convolutional channel projections, Squeeze-and-Excitation (SE) calibrations, and Transformer-based geometric self-attention, we achieve leading baseline inference ($R^2=0.826$) while stripping recurrent network parameter footprints by $88\%$.
    \item \textbf{Physics-Informed Deep Learning}: We regularize backpropagation by injecting the classic fatigue \textit{Paris Law} ($da/dN = C \cdot \Delta K^m$) dynamically into the loss function, mathematically prohibiting non-physical "structural recovery" predictions and reducing critical RUL extrapolation error by 23\%.
    \item \textbf{Multi-Objective Design Discovery}: Implementation of scalarized ParEGO Bayesian Optimization atop Gaussian Process (GP) surrogates to simultaneously maximize RUL, stiffness mapping, and strength retention across an infinite CFRP layup design space.
\end{enumerate}

\subsection{Architectural Advancements in Sequence Modelling}
We rigorously evaluate a stack of diverse neural networks structurally modified for localized sequence capability, notably designing an entirely novel paradigm (HybridSTA) specifically tailored for geometric piezo arrays:
\begin{enumerate}
    \item \textbf{Temporal Convolutional Networks (TCN)}: Leveraging $d=2^i$ exponentially dilated causal padding loops to establish robust horizon analysis bounds.
    \item \textbf{BiLSTM with Attention Gates}: Combining bi-directional hidden Markov paths paired with temporal focus to identify sudden hysteresis non-linearities commonly denoting Phase-II micro-crack saturations.
    \item \textbf{Hybrid Spatial Temporal Attention (HybridSTA)}: Our novel architecture. It uniquely isolates feature scaling via sequence-wise Squeeze-and-Excitation (SE) mechanisms before flowing representations into a deep multi-head Transformer encoder equipped with a geometric $(x, y)$ coordinate-aligned structural formulation. It learns discrete inter-channel relationships (Eq \ref{eq:attention}) across the true physical $4 \times 4$ layout without parameter bloat.
\end{enumerate}

\section{Physics-Informed Bounds and Uncertainty}
%═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Pure empirical models routinely produce non-physical outputs when presented with unknown out-of-distribution (OOD) testing scenarios. 

\subsection{Physics-Informed Neural Network (PINN)}
To combat non-physical "stiffness recovery" predictions natively generated by purely data-driven ML frameworks, we force convergence into a kinetically constrained manifold governed by a discrete, automated derivative of the generalized \textit{Paris Law}. 
\begin{equation}
\mathcal{L}_{\text{total}} = \mathcal{L}_{\text{data}} + \lambda \cdot \text{ReLU}\left(\frac{\partial E}{\partial N} - \left[ C(\Delta K)^m \right] \right)
\label{eq:pinn_loss}
\end{equation}

By actively penalizing predictions that randomly violate theoretical strictly monotonic loss properties via PyTorch's computational autograd topology, the PINN actively restricts mathematical divergence. This constrained boundary ensures deep learning stability precisely throughout the critical final $20\%$ of lifecycle advanced failure epochs (Fig. \ref{fig:pinn}).

\section{Optimization Engine Discovery}
%═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Identifying optimal structural composite designs necessitates exploring an exponentially vast layup topology sequence ($[0^\circ, \pm 15^\circ, \pm 30^\circ ...]$) mapped to performance criteria.  

We harness a multidimensional Gaussian Process (GP) Surrogate paired with a scalarized Expected Improvement (EI) heuristic to automate Bayesian Material Informatics discovery.
\begin{equation}
EI(\mathbf{x}) = (\mu(\mathbf{x}) - f_{\text{best}} - \xi)\Phi(Z) + \sigma(\mathbf{x})\phi(Z)
\end{equation}

The heuristic effectively bounds trade-offs between Remaining Useful Life prediction ($max$), stiffness modulus retention, and normalized directional strength efficiency ($\cos^2(\theta)$) constraints. Over extensive matrix sampling, the advanced structural Bayesian loop evaluates an exact, geometrically stable \textbf{Pareto Front} objective landscape.
